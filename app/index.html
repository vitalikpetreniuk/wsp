ABOUT US

Managing Opportunities. Preserving Wealth. Building Legacies.

The financial planning services we offer are holistic, ensuring that no element of your finances are left undone. What We Offer

Trusting someone with your financial legacy is a big decision. At WSP, we understand that wealth management goes far beyond your here and now. You want to feel confident in your family’s future, regardless of life’s complications. That’s why our team of advisors is dedicated to providing the highest quality of care and always keeping your family’s unique financial goals in mind when giving counsel. Through our tailored financial strategy, our team helps you manage opportunities, preserve wealth, and build a legacy for generations to come.

Though our offices are in Nashville, TN, and Sarasota, FL, we service families across the United States.
