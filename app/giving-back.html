Our Process

We care for
our communities.

At Wealth Strategies Partners, we believe that our financial house extends beyond our families and into the larger communities in which we belong. That’s why WSP sponsors charitable organizations and causes that are important to our clients and our team. Our community investment is primarily focused on organizations that promote education, support the arts, and serve the disabled.

Do you belong to a non-profit organization and want to apply for a grant? We invite you to review our requirements and process below.

Our Giving Back Process

  • 1 Contact us

    We invite you to contact us by making an inquiry in the form below, and then we’ll send you your request form.

  • 2 Complete your request form

    Next, you’ll fill out the form and follow its instructions to send it to us. Organizations may also mail hard copies if desired.

  • 3 Check your email for our response!

    Grant requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis and all organizations will be notified via email of our decision after its submission has been reviewed. We look forward to hearing from you.

REQUIREMENTS

Organization Requirements

  • We only support non-profit organizations with a 501(c)(3) designation or accredited schools and universities.
  • Eligible groups include those primarily focused on education, the arts, and helping those with disabilities.
  • Organizations must be located in and/or serve those living in Tennessee or Florida.
