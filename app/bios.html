The Forbes ranking of Best-In-State Wealth Advisors, developed by SHOOK Research, is based on an algorithm of qualitative criteria, mostly gained through telephone and in-person due diligence interviews, and quantitative data. Paul C. Allen, CFP®, MS

President
  • paul@wealthstrategiespartners.com
  • (617) 723-5508

Paul is the president of Wealth Strategies Partners and a financial executive with more than three decades of industry experience. Paul founded Wealth Strategies Partners in 2014 to provide comprehensive, white glove financial planning services to retirees, entrepreneurs, business owners, and executives. He and his experienced team work with clients on an individual basis to create customized, strategic, financial plans that are tailored to their unique needs.

Paul earned his bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of Tennessee and subsequently earned his master’s degree in personal financial planning from the College for Financial Planning. He went on to hold executive positions at global financial firms, including Prudential Securities and Aintree Capital, LLC, where he rose to the role of vice president.

He was named a Best-in-State Wealth Advisor by Forbes magazine in 2018, 2021 and 2022. He was also named a member of the Raymond James Financial Services’ Chairman’s Council, a distinction given only to those financial advisors who have demonstrated an unparalleled commitment to personal service and lead the largest revenue-producing branches, in 2019, 2020, and 2021. He has achieved the Certified Financial Planner™ certification, which is earned after completing a rigorous CFP® exam and meeting ongoing certification renewal requirements.

A Nashville native, Paul splits his time between the practice’s offices in Nashville and Sarasota. He believes in investing in the community and currently serves as a Circle Member at the Ringling Art Museum in Sarasota. He is also a member of the Pi Kappa Alpha Alumni Association, Alpha Pi Chapter, and attends Spero Dei Church in Nashville.
